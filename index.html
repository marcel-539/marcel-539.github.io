FILE NEMO is the digital version of Point Nemo.
Point Nemo is the furthest point in the ocean from any land.
It is located in the South Pacific and was calculated in 1992.
Point Nemo serves as a spaceship graveyard, as decommissioned satellites
and space stations are intentionally crashed in this area.
FILE NEMO serves as a data graveyard and offers the possibility of
uploading files that are no longer needed and disposing them
into the depths of the digital ocean.
