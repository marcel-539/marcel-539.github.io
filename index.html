TEXT NEMO

TEXT NEMO is the digital version of Point Nemo.
Point Nemo is the furthest point in the ocean from any land.
It is located in the South Pacific and was calculated in 1992.
Point Nemo serves as a spaceship graveyard, as decommissioned satellites and space stations are intentionally crashed in this area.

TEXT NEMO serves as a digital text graveyard and offers the possibility of uploading thoughts, ideas, love letters and other messages that nobody and everybody should read.
Every published text disappears after a certain time.
The longer the text, the longer it remains visible and the deeper it sinks.
